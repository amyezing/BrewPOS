Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026
Short version: BrewPOS stores all your data locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with any server or third party.

1. Who We Are

BrewPOS is a point-of-sale application designed for bubble tea shops and cafés. The app is developed and maintained by an independent developer.

Contact: amyezinggamer@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

BrewPOS does not collect any personal data from users. All data entered into the app is stored exclusively on your device using the browser's built-in IndexedDB storage.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Store settings (store name, address, payment details)
  • Product catalog and pricing
  • Ingredient inventory levels
  • Order history and receipts
  • Customer names and phone numbers (loyalty program)
  • Product photos you upload

None of this data leaves your device. It is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identification
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not use advertising SDKs
  • We do not access your contacts, location, or call log
  • We do not store any data on external servers
  • We do not share any data with third parties

4. Camera and Photo Access

BrewPOS may request access to your device's camera or photo gallery to allow you to upload product images. Photos you choose to upload are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted externally.

5. Customer Data (Loyalty Program)

If you use the loyalty program feature, you may enter customer names and phone numbers. This data is:

  • Stored only on the device running BrewPOS
  • Never transmitted to any external server
  • Fully under your control as the store owner
  • Deletable at any time via the Customers section or the "Clear All Data" option in Settings

As the store owner, you are responsible for informing your customers that their information is recorded for loyalty purposes and for complying with applicable local data protection laws.

6. Internet Access

BrewPOS is a Progressive Web App (PWA). It requires internet access only for the initial download. Once installed, it operates fully offline. The app may connect to the internet to:

  • Load Google Fonts for typography
  • Check for app updates via the service worker

No personal or store data is ever sent during these connections.

7. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored using your device's IndexedDB, which is sandboxed to the app and inaccessible to other apps or websites. Data persists until you uninstall the app or manually clear it via Settings → Clear All Data.

8. Children's Privacy

BrewPOS is a business tool intended for use by adults operating food and beverage businesses. It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us:
